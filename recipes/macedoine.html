Macedoine salad

It is a mixture of vegetables, some of which are diced. It is usually made with carrots, turnips, green beans and peas my this recipe is with rice

ingredients

  • 250 g carrots
  • 150 g of turnips
  • 100 g green beans
  • 100 g shelled peas
  • mayonnaise
  • 2-3 of Tomato or cherry tomato
  • a packet of lettuce
  • rice

insructions

  1. steam the rice
  2. slice Turnips and carrots then cook them in water
  3. cook the peas and green beans then slice them
  4. After cooking, drain the vegetables and immediately refresh them in very cold water to stop their cooking and preserve the color.
  5. them in a colander and place them on paper towels.
  6. in a bowl and gently mixe the vegetables and rice
  7. add mayonnaise , salt and a lil' vinegar and mix
  8. Cook hard-boiled eggs to accompany this mixture.
  9. To serve, mold in a circle or in a small bowl. Add a few salad leaves, tomatoes and hard-boiled eggs cut in half or into quarters.
