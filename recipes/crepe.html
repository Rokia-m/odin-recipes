easy french crepe

This simple but delicious crÃªpe recipe can be made in minutes from ingredients that everyone has on hand

ingrediants

  • 1 cup all purpose flour

  • 1 tbsp sugar

  • salt

  • 2 ~3 eggs

  • 1 and 1/2 cup of milk

  • 1 tbsp butter

insructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk flour, granulated sugar, and salt to combine. Create a well in center of dry ingredients. Crack eggs into well. Gradually pour in milk, whisking from the center to incorporate dry ingredients. Let stand at room temperature until slightly bubbly on top, 15 to 20 minutes.

  2. In a small skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Drop about 1/4 cup batter into pan, then swirl pan to coat bottom in a thin, even layer.

  3. Cook until just set, about 2 minutes, then flip and continue to cook until second side is golden brown, about 1 minute more. Repeat with remaining batter.

  4. add you toping ! you can topit with fruit or chocolet
